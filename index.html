Financial Advisor and Trading Platform

FinSight is an AI-driven financial analytics and virtual trading platform that integrates real-time market data with quantitative risk metrics. It supports risk-aware investment decisions through analytics, asset classification, and simulated trading.

Problem Statement

Retail investors increasingly access digital trading platforms but often lack proper risk understanding and structured decision support. There is a need for an integrated system that combines analytics, risk evaluation, and safe trading simulation.

Literature Review / Market Research

Existing platforms focus separately on trading execution, analytics, education, or simulation. Few solutions integrate risk-adjusted metrics into a unified framework.

Research Gap / Innovation

FinSight bridges the gap by combining real-time analytics, quantitative risk classification, AI-based explanations, and paper trading within a single intelligent system.

SYSTEM METHODOLOGY

Dataset / Input

Publicly available financial time-series data from Yahoo Finance including stocks, indices, mutual funds, and commodities. Data consists of daily closing prices, dates, and USD–INR rates. Preprocessing includes cleaning, alignment, normalization, and return calculation.

Model / Architecture

Modular architecture: Data Acquisition → Preprocessing → Financial Metrics (Returns, Volatility, Sharpe, MDD, CAGR) → Risk Classification → Visualization → AI Explanation → Paper Trading Simulation.
